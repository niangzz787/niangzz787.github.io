About Metro Boomin

Metro Boomin (Leland Tyler Wayne, born September 16, 1993) is a 32-year-old American record producer, DJ, and songwriter who has defined the modern trap sound.

Known for his dark, cinematic production, he has created massive hits for artists like Future, Gunna, Young Thug, Drake, and The Weeknd.

Born in St. Louis, Missouri, he began his career while in high school, traveling to Atlanta to work with artists like OJ Da Juiceman and Future.

The Song that would rise him to stardom would be Bad and Boujee featuring Migos

Trap
The word “trap” originally refers to a place where drugs are sold (a “trap house”).So trap music often reflects themes of:Street life Hustling, Survival, Poverty, Crime, and Success after struggle
